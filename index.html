Terraria Mods




Pet Crystals
You're able to convert the normal pet summon items into crystals,
that you can use as an item to spawn and despawn the pets (don't>
worry, the pets won't be made of crystals or anything), and the
dye in the pet slot even affects them.


Untold Legends
Untold Legends is a Work-In-Progress mod that adds in Skill Trees!
So far there is only the Ranged one, but more will come in the future!


Exitium's Quality of Life Pack
Exitium's Quality of Life Pack contains multiple things that can all be toggled
on and off, specifics are on the mod's description on the Mod Browser
